\section{Executive Summary}

This document presents a comprehensive financial analysis and investment opportunity for Fractal Finance's consumer fintech platform. We are seeking \$350,000 in pre-seed funding to execute our 12-month Phase 1 strategy, with equity terms to be negotiated. Our innovative prepaid card offering provides unprecedented control over user spending habits while generating interchange revenue.

\subsection{Three-Phase Strategy}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
\item \textbf{Phase 1A (Months 1-6):} Beta testing with no monetization, focus on product-market fit
\item \textbf{Phase 1B (Months 7-12):} Public launch with virtual prepaid card rollout, 1.25\% cashback for all users
\item \textbf{Phase 2 (Months 13-24):} Full monetization launch with premium tier (1.25\% cashback) and free tier (1\% cashback)
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Key Investment Highlights}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
\item \textbf{Market Opportunity:} Targeting consumer financial management with validated \$9.99/month premium pricing
\item \textbf{Virtual Prepaid Card Innovation:} Industry-first spending control via transaction blocking with 0.4\% interchange revenue
\item \textbf{Phased Approach:} De-risked strategy with product validation before monetization
\item \textbf{Capital Efficiency:} \$350,000 funds complete Phase 1 (12 months) including 19.6\% buffer
\item \textbf{Enhanced Unit Economics:} Virtual prepaid cards increase conversion by 2.5x and add interchange revenue stream
\item \textbf{Content-First UA Strategy:} Multi-channel approach prioritizing sustainable growth channels
\item \textbf{Competitive Advantage:} Hard spending limits via prepaid cards drive superior retention and user satisfaction
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Investment Terms}
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Funding Ask:} \$350,000 upfront investment
\item \textbf{Use of Proceeds:} 12-month runway covering all Phase 1 operations
\item \textbf{Equity:} To be negotiated based on investor interest and strategic value
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Phase 2 Unit Economics}

\textbf{Key Formulas:}
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{CAC} = Cost Per Install (CPI) ÷ Combined Premium Conversion Rate
\item \textbf{LTV} = (Subscription Revenue × Margin × Customer Lifetime) + (Card Revenue × Customer Lifetime) - Card Issuance Cost
\item \textbf{Customer Lifetime} = 1 ÷ Monthly Churn Rate = 1 ÷ 3.5\% = 28.6 months
\item \textbf{Combined Conversion} = Install→Registration × Registration→Card × Card→Premium
\end{itemize}

\begin{table}[h]

\textbf{LTV Calculation Breakdown:} 
\begin{itemize}
\item Premium subscription: \$9.99/month
\item Gross margin: 85\% (typical SaaS margins)
\item Monthly revenue after margin: \$9.99 × 85\% = \$8.49
\item Customer lifetime: 1 ÷ 3.5\% = 28.6 months
\item Subscription LTV: \$8.49 × 28.6 = \$243
\item Average card spend: \$1200/month
\item Interchange revenue: \$1200 × 0.4\% = \$4.80/month
\item Cashback cost: \$1200 × 1.25\% = \$15.00/month (blended rate)
\item Net card loss per month: -\$10.20
\item Lifetime card contribution: -\$10.20 × 28.6 = -\$292.92
\item Total LTV: \$243 - \$292.92 = -\$50
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Strategic Value of Cards Despite Negative Unit Economics:}
\begin{itemize}

\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Risk:} Lower than expected 75\% card adoption rate
\item \textbf{Mitigation:} 1.25\% cashback incentive and unique spending controls drive adoption; virtual cards have lower barrier to entry than physical cards
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Cashback Economics Risk:}
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Risk:} Higher spending than \$650/month average increases cashback costs
\item \textbf{Mitigation:} Prepaid nature naturally limits spending; can adjust cashback rates if needed; spending caps possible
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Technical Risk:}
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Risk:} Card platform integration delays or issues
\item \textbf{Mitigation:} Lithic provides robust APIs and support; 3-month integration buffer in Phase 1A; virtual cards simpler to implement than physical
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Phase Transition Risk}

\textbf{Monetization Risk:}
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Risk:} Conversion to premium lower than projected 10.4-17.6\%

\end{itemize}

\textbf{Regulatory Risk:}
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Risk:} Prepaid card regulations change
\item \textbf{Mitigation:} Working with established BaaS provider (Lithic) ensures compliance; Canadian regulations stable
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Funding Risk:}
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Risk:} Difficulty raising Phase 2 funding with negative card economics
\item \textbf{Mitigation:} Focus on conversion metrics and LTV growth trajectory; 13.7\% buffer extends runway if needed

\textbf{Competition Risk:}
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Risk:} Banks launch competing spend control cards
\item \textbf{Mitigation:} First-mover advantage with automation features; superior UX focus; prepaid structure unique
\end{itemize}
